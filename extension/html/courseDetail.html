• CP 476 - Internet Computing

    The architecture of the Internet. Client-server programming, technologies of the web (URLs, HTML, HTTP, applets, etc.) Introduction to building web applications and server-side programming.

    Prerequisites: CP363, and either CP372 (co-requisite) or CP364.
    Co-requisites: CP372.

    Credits: 0.50
    Lecture hours: 3.00

    Academic level: Undergraduate

    Faculty: Faculty of Science
    Department: Physics & Computer Science

    Restrictions:
    Must be enrolled in one of the following Levels:     
          Undergraduate
    May not be enrolled as the following Classifications:     
          Not Applicable
          Year 1

    Prerequisites:
    Undergraduate level CP 363 Minimum Grade of D- and (Undergraduate level CP 372 Minimum Grade of D- or Undergraduate level CP 364 Minimum Grade of D-)
